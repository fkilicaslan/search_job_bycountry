Areas of ownership: Lead and mentor a team of Business Development Executives to achieve and exceed sales targets in the US market. Drive collaboration and communication across the organization to achieve collective success and deliver exceptional customer value. Strong analytical skills with the ability to derive insights from sales data. Ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams and drive results in a fast-paced environment.
