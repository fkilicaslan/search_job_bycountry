HUADE HYDROGEN | HYESS-R System Location: Germany (Remote/Field-based) Industry: Renewable Energy, Hydrogen, HVAC & Storage Target Markets: Residential & Small Commercial (B2B/B2B2C) Role Overview We are seeking a highly experienced Senior Sales Manager to drive the market penetration of our HYESS-R residential hydrogen energy storage system in Germany. This role is designed for a sales professional who not only understands hardware but is an expert in the intersection of Solar PV, Battery Storage (ESS), and Heating/HVAC ecosystems. Mandatory Professional Background Candidates MUST possess a proven track record in the following areas to be considered: · Residential Solar PV & Battery Storage: Deep expertise in selling inverters, lithium-ion battery systems, and complete residential PV solutions. · Heating & HVAC Market (SHK): Proven experience in the German heating market, including heat pumps or Micro-CHP systems. Understanding the integration of energy storage with household thermal management is crucial. · Market Rules & Regulatory Expertise: A comprehensive understanding of the German energy market rules, including grid connection standards (VDE), the Renewable Energy Sources Act (EEG), and local subsidy frameworks (KFW, etc.). · Established Network: An active and verified network of German PV distributors, specialized HVAC wholesalers, and master-installers (Meisterbetriebe). Key Responsibilities · Strategic Channel Expansion: Identify and secure partnerships with German distributors and heating system integrators to roll out the HyESS-R system. · Complex Solution Selling: Articulate the unique value of hydrogen-based seasonal storage versus traditional lithium-only systems to sophisticated B2B partners. · Sales Cycle Management: Manage the full sales lifecycle from prospecting and technical consultation to contract negotiation and post-sales channel support. · Market Compliance: Ensure all sales activities align with German technical standards and energy regulations, acting as a bridge between HQ R&D and the local market needs. · Brand Positioning: Position HyESS-R as the premium choice for 100% energy independence and decarbonized heating in the German premium residential sector.
