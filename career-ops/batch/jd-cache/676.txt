Job Application for Majors Account Executive, Berlin at Vercel Back to jobs Majors Account Executive, Berlin Hybrid - Berlin Apply About Vercel: Vercel gives developers the tools and cloud infrastructure to build, scale, and secure a faster, more personalized web. As the team behind v0, Next.js, and AI SDK, Vercel helps customers like Ramp, Supreme, PayPal, and Under Armour build for the AI-native web. Our mission is to enable the world to ship the best products. That starts with creating a place where everyone can do their best work. Whether you're building on our platform, supporting our customers, or shaping our story: You can just ship things. About the Role: Vercel's sales team is filled with hardworking, deeply technical people spanning a range of business functions (developers, operations, marketing, product, IT) to help create a new category. We are a fast-growing organization with a strong preference to grow team members and promote from within! You will be the dominant driver of revenue growth and be on the front- lines of evangelizing our platform to both new and existing customers. If you’re based within a pre-determined commuting distance of one of our offices (SF, NY, London, or Berlin), the role includes in-office anchor days on Monday, Tuesday, and Friday. If you're located beyond that distance, the role is fully remote. For location-specific details, please connect with our recruiting team. What You Will Do: Build and execute multi-year account plans across a curated set of named Major Accounts in DACH, with cloud, SI, and agency partners embedded in the strategy from day one Develop and activate partner relationships to source pipeline, co-sell into accounts, and accelerate deal cycles Drive net-new revenue through outbound ownership, deep discovery, executive alignment, and multi-threaded deal execution spanning engineering, product, procurement, legal, and the C-suite Translate Vercel's platform into board-level narratives around developer productivity, infrastructure consolidation, and AI transformation as operational levers Own complex commercial negotiations and position Vercel as a long-term strategic infrastructure partner embedded in your accounts' roadmaps for web and AI transformation About You: 5-10+ years enterprise SaaS, with a track record of closing six and seven-figure deals in major or strategic accounts Experienced working a partner-influenced or co-sell motion — you know how to activate a hyperscaler or SI/ISV relationship to open doors and accelerate deals, not just list them as references Fluent in German and English; equally comfortable with a CTO on architecture and a CFO on TCO A disciplined account planner who thinks in years, builds executive relationships from scratch, and sequences complex accounts to maximise long-term revenue Fluent in MEDDPICC Genuinely able to articulate AI and modern web technology - enough to lead credible conversations with senior engineering and product leadership without leaning on a solutions engineer initially A value seller who leads with business outcomes, not feature lists A disciplined forecaster and curator of tight systems hygiene Bonus If You: Have a background in web development, developer tooling, web infrastructure, cloud, or AI/ML platforms Come equipped with existing relationships with AWS, or other major SI/ISV players active in DACH Have experience navigating ELAs, global framework agreements, or regulated industry procurement in the DACH market Benefits: Great compensation package and stock options.
