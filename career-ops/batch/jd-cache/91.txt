We are looking for: Sales Manager Automotive Main responsibilities : Drive sustainable business growth by owning and managing the full sales cycle—from lead generation and opportunity identification to deal negotiation and closing. Represent the company at customer meetings, industry events, and trade shows. Required qualifications : Demonstrated experience managing and growing relationships with customers that have a global footprint. Strong track record of working with OEMs and Tier1 suppliers, understanding their processes and requirements. Knowledge of cellular communication technologies is an advantage. Proven ability to collaborate effectively in international, cross-functional teams. 3+ years of experience in automotive sales, successfully driving business growth and managing complex sales cycles.
