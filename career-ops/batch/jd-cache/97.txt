Direct message the job poster from Service Club ® Jean Jimenez Jean Jimenez Talent Acquisition Specialist at SERVICE CLUB About Service Club At Service Club , we are on a mission to make hiring in the service industry smarter, faster, and more sustainable. We sell a smart Jobs platform and an award-winning Academy directly to logistics and delivery fleets. Backed by funding from top-tier investors including Banco Sabadell, and Mutua Madrileña, we are a high-growth startup revolutionizing how the UK and EU workforce moves. The German gig economy is battling unprecedented driver shortages and massive churn, and we solve problems that traditional recruitment simply can't touch. We empower top brands to find, train, and onboard pre-vetted drivers quickly, while helping jobseekers upskill and succeed. As a proud winner of the United Nations & SantanderX Global Start-Up Challenges, we are scaling our revenue engine and building the undisputed industry standard. The Role We are looking for our first boots on the ground in Germany. As our Country Sales Manager for the German market you will lead our revenue growth in a massive, untapped market. You will work directly with the Head of Global Sales & CEO to define our approach and own outcomes. You are a curious pure hunter and closer. You will take highly qualified leads generated by our Growth Ops team and run them to closed-won, while simultaneously hunting your own mid-market and enterprise accounts. Your focus is penetrating the German market, driving revenue, and establishing Service Club as the undisputed industry standard. You have a contemporary sales mindset, equipped to use technology to aid your sales process. What You Will Do Own the Full Cycle: Drive the end-to-end enterprise sales process, from targeted outbound prospecting and deep discovery to complex negotiations and closing. Strategic Hunting & Pipeline Building: Partner with the Head of Global Sales and Growth Ops to convert intent signals, while relentlessly hunting your own Tier 1 target accounts to turn market intelligence into signed contracts. Multi-Thread Deals: Navigate complex buying committees by aligning different stakeholders (Operations, HR, Procurement) around a unified business case. Sell on Value: Shift the conversation away from transactional pricing. Focus heavily on operational ROI and solving deep business pains for logistics leaders. Lead the Room: Command discovery calls and strategic product demonstrations for C-level executives. Control the Momentum: Drive urgency, manage pilot conversions, and push through procurements without letting deals stall. Forecasting Discipline: Maintain pipeline hygiene in HubSpot to provide leadership with highly accurate, data-driven revenue forecasts. What You Need The Experience: 4+ years of B2B closing experience, specifically handling mid-market and enterprise sales cycles in a SaaS or high-growth tech environment. The Vertical Edge: A strong understanding of the Logistics, Supply Chain, or Fleet Management sectors, alongside a network you can leverage from day one. The Track Record: A verifiable history of beating quota and closing six-figure deals. The Hunter Mentality: You do not sit back and wait for leads. You know how to break into cold accounts, multi-thread and build your own book of business. The Methodology: Mastery of a proven sales framework (MEDDIC, BANT, SPIN, Sandler, etc.) to qualify deals and avoid inaccurate pipelines. The Presence: Native-level German with the curiosity and mindset to challenge, consult, and negotiate with operational leaders. The Scale-up Mindset: You thrive in high-velocity environments where you have to build the plane while flying it and enjoy seeing your solutions and suggestions come to life. What We Offer Highly Competitive Compensation: A strong base salary plus an aggressive commission structure on high value deals (specifics discussed in your first interview). 100% Remote Flexibility: Work from wherever you are most productive in Germany, giving you the autonomy to structure your day for maximum impact. Industry Presence: Opportunities to travel, attend, and represent Service Club at premier German industry events and trade shows. Meaningful Impact: The chance to be a foundational piece in a unique, mission-driven company solving critical, real-world bottlenecks. Viva Spain: Join the team in Barcelona for company events. At the Service Club, talent comes first. We are committed to equal opportunity employment, regardless of race, color, religion, gender, sexual orientation, age, gender identity, or disability. If you are ready to lead Sales at a high-growth startup and make a real impact, we want to hear from you. Apply now and join us in shaping the future of workforce mobility.
